Cosgreat Trading is building next-generation AI solutions across FinTech, automation, SaaS, and AI agents.

The company is looking for ambitious interns who want hands-on experience building real products instead of only doing routine internship tasks.

Formal IT certification is not required. Candidates from traditional and non-traditional backgrounds are welcome if they can demonstrate logical thinking, fast learning ability, initiative, and genuine interest in AI and software development.

Interns will work closely with the development team to design, build, test, and deploy AI-powered solutions that solve real business problems.

The internship is suitable for candidates who want to grow into an AI Engineer or AI Solutions Architect career path.

High performers may be offered a full-time role during or after the internship.
