Question: What is RAG?
Answer: RAG means Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It helps an AI system answer questions using external knowledge. The system first retrieves relevant information from a knowledge base, then uses that context to generate a more grounded answer.

Question: Why is RAG useful for companies?
Answer: RAG is useful because companies often have private, updated, or domain-specific information that a normal language model may not know. RAG helps the model answer based on internal documents and reduces hallucination.
